Welcome to Hats & Co.

The Hats and Co hat shop in Regent Street, Leamington Spa is owned by sisters Erica Smith and Pam Goddard. It is managed by the owners and dedicated staff who adore hats! Hats and Co is the perfect place to find the perfect hat or fascinator at the best price.
