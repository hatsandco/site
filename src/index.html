Welcome to Hats & Co.

Hiring a hat for a special occasion couldn't be easier, go to Hats & Co.

The Hats and Co hat shop in Regent Street, Leamington Spa is owned by sisters Erica Smith and Pam Goddard. It is managed by the owners and dedicated staff who adore hats! Hats and Co is the perfect place to find the perfect hat, headpiece or fascinator at the best price.

We stock only British-made designer hats, from milliners we trust, such as Vivien Sheriff, Rebecca Couture, Max & Ellie, Darcie Mae & Whiteleys. We have only one of any piece in stock, making it special for you.

If you're looking for the perfect accessory for your outfit, we also sell handbags, wraps for the summer and gloves & scarves in the winter.
